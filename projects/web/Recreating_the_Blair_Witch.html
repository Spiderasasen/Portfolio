Project:
Recreating the Blair Witch ARG

In this project, I built a replica of the infamous Blair Witch ARG, aiming to capture the eerie atmosphere and bring back the unsettling moments people experienced back then. It’s not just a 1‑to‑1 recreation — I also added a new mini‑story to make the experience unique.
The project uses the three core web languages: HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. With HTML, I explored new elements like image mapping. CSS made me fall in love with hover effects, which turned out to be incredibly useful for buttons and visual feedback. JavaScript, while confusing at first, helped me deepen my understanding of logic and interactivity.
Working on this project didn’t just improve my technical skills — it made me enjoy programming even more. Finishing it gave me confidence and taught me how much I can learn by experimenting and pushing through challenges.
