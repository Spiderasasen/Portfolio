Project:
Ciphers

I’ve always loved a good cipher — it’s what I used in my Blair Witch Recreation. Ciphers challenge people to uncover hidden messages, whether it’s something simple like a Caesar cipher or something nearly impossible, like the one I used in the Reincarnation section of my ARG (a cipher introduced by NASA on a Mars rover). This project focuses on encrypting and decrypting many simple ciphers. More complex ones require brute force or the right system to crack. Originally, this was going to be part of another ARG I was building with a friend, but college life put that on hold. Still, I’m proud of this work and plan to update it in the future to make it even cleaner and more polished.
