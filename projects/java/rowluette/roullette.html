Project
Roulette

WHO LOVES ROULETTE!!! This is a simple game: place your bet and watch the “ball” spin. Nothing complicated. So why build it? I wanted to learn how timers work. Timers are amazing because they can raise the stakes and make code feel more dynamic. Sure, a timer that just counts from 1 to 10 over and over is boring, but tying it to a game makes it fun and engaging. This project let me experiment with timers in a playful way while building something interactive.
